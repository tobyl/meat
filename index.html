What?

A meatfest. THE meatfest. The meatfest to end all meatfests.

2018 was a simpler time. No pandemic. Russia had only invaded Ukraine once or twice. And the first meat party took place.

Now it's time for round two. Meat II: The Porkening will be a celebration of meat of all kinds.

More meat. More fire. More tractor. More hot tub. Bring a bathing suit!

Still on the fence? Anxious about spending more than 30 minutes talking to other human beings? Fear not! There'll be activities:

If you're reading this, then you. Possibly. It's a public site, so who knows.
